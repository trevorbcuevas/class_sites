Lesson 01 · Budgeting
The Myth
"More money will fix being broke."
Wrong. If you can't manage what you have now, a bigger paycheck just means bigger problems. Budgeting is the skill that actually keeps money in your pocket, at any income level.

Lesson 02 · Investing
Stop Waiting
It's not about timing the market.
It's about time in the market. Trying to find the perfect moment is a losing game. People who build real wealth start early and stay consistent, compound interest does the rest.

Lesson 03 · Safety Net
The Boring One
Your emergency fund is your freedom.
Life will throw something at you. Without 3–6 months of expenses saved, one bad day becomes months of debt. It's not exciting, it's what keeps everything else from falling apart.
